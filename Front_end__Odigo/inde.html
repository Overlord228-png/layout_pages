In 1945, at the end of World War II, the command of the US armed forces chose this city as a target for a nuclear strike. The Governor of Niigata Prefecture ordered people to be evacuated due to rumors of an impending bombardment, and the city was completely abandoned for several days. However, due to bad weather and distance from the B-29 base in the Marianas, the city was removed from the list of targets; the bomb was dropped on Nagasaki.
